\begin{frame}
   {I2C devices on Linux}
   \begin{itemize}
      \item From the Linux userspace, you can access the I2C bus
	      from the \verb?/dev/i2c-*? device files
      \begin{raw}
pi@raspberrypi:~$ ls -l /dev/i2c-1 
crw-rw---- 1 root i2c 89, 1 Jan 20 19:47 /dev/i2c-1
      \end{raw}
   \end{itemize}
\end{frame}
